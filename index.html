APVBS Activities

(A Mission for Valmiki Community Unity, Development and Empowerment). 

APVBS is organized and will be operated exclusively for Educational, Health, Literacy promoting, Social Services for the needy Valmiki Boya (Orphans, Women, Children, Disabled & Old Age) and in furtherance thereof, specific objectives are adopted like (To promote an increased Education, Health & Social Services to the Needy (Orphans, Women, Children, Disabled & Old Age), To promote a greater interest in Social Service & Welfare Activities and its applications, To provide a means of communication between persons having interest in Social Service & Welfare Activities Additional objectives constitute every nook & corner that emphasizes the Development & welfare of the Needy & Poor Valmiki Boya).
To cooperate with government in all its activities to eradicate medical, health, family welfare and educational problems of Valmiki Boya.
Provide health services to the rural and urban people, medical care, mother care, child care, family planning health education, prevention and control of diseases and other rehabilitation services and family counselling exclusively for Valmiki Boya.
To promote Valmiki Boya's agriculture developmental activities in backward-draught areas and literacy enhancement in farmers regarding various government schemes for their empowerment.
Development of Valmiki Boya handicapped, differently disabled persons in rural and urban areas.
Valmiki Boya Youth empowering ideas by encouraging them in Indoor/outdoor sports, games, and personality development programs to serve the nation.
Competitive Coaching initiatives for Valmiki Boya backward students to get jobs and career guidance programs to increase Employment and reduce Unemployment ratios.
Mobile and Standard permanent library initiatives for education and literacy promotion in all nearby villages for Valmiki Boya.
To visit orphanages, Old age homes for their need and strive for their basic needs and in future we try our best to establish homes respectively for Valmiki Boya.
To promote economic balance of the society in all ways and strive for the rural backward people enhancement by obtaining contracts from central and state governments, public bodies or individuals or by executives and with the help of our Valmiki Boya Community members.
Valmiki Boya Community halls establishment through our Sangam.
Valmiki Boya Self-help groups for the rural mahila empowerment.
We strive for the Valmiki Boya people and sangam in all ways what we can do and help the government bodies in whatever ways they are in need.

"Services Rendering to the People" on behalf of

APVBS Andhra Pradesh Valmiki Boya Sangam.

Taking Different Initiatives to attract Investments in the Region where all Mandals were Draught declared and doesn’t have even one investment.
Taking Initiatives to create different types of Channels to Connect State and District Head Quarter in all ways like Education, Employment, Health and Other requirements for the People in region as it was Very backward and doesn’t have many Facilities.
Conducting Medical Health Camps for the Poor and Needy in Draught and Remote areas of the Constituency and around Kurnool District.
Conducting Camps on Water Conversation and effective use of it for farming and etc..,
Taking some initiatives to make region Friendly to setup Government and Private Institutions to attract different types of other related Investments.
Conducting different types of camps on Education, Literacy Promotion, and Public Policy Schemes in near villages by empowering them how to get public schemes in a hassle free way and what are the different schemes available for them etc..,
 Conducting Social Welfare camps to eradicate migration in Rural Backward areas and enlightening them with the new methods of cultivation to avoid crop loss and Schemes to Use to get benefit while they face loss.
Joining the Poor Students and Rejoining the Dropouts in near residential schools and counseling the remote villagers about compulsory education and benefits of Education who are facing lack of development in many ways due to no type of profession.
Taking initiatives in the name of Village Comeback those who are settled in towns and Cities due to worst conditions of region to come back to Villages and Participate in the Village Development and Empowerment Activities through different ways.
